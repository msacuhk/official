LIFE 2021: Career Sharing

27 Mar 2021

Suceeding LIFE 1001 in the last academic year, the LIFE 2001 career sharing is designed to equipt participants with know-hows of planning their future career pathways and giving them insights on planning ahead for their post-graduation life.

4 guest speakers have been invited to share their working and research experiences both post-graduation and also during undergraduate years. They are: PACC graduate who is currently working in one of the Big 4 accounting firms, MPhil student whose chosen research field is neuroscience, GLEF student who has co-founded a start-up on anti-microbial phone case, and a passionate alumnus who is currently working at a start-up as under a project management role. They shared on topics of working cultures in Hong Kong, the research journey of an academia, tips to establish a start-up and important factors to be considered when choosing a career.

Participants had responded that they found the session was informative, insightful and inspiring. We hoped that this has helped participants in making early preparation for their future endeavors.
