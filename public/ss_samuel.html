I fell in love with the campus when I first visited in 2010 as there is a lot of greenery, reading rooms overlooking the sea, and the overall high level of maintenance of facilities. When I returned in 2018 as a student, CUHK brought back all the best memories of studying at UM in the early 2000s, and the positive vibes amongst the students made even old students like myself (I was 38) feel deeply engaged. I enjoyed the experience so much that I decided to further my PhD, and although it's been four years, there is always something new that makes me feel proud to say that I have walked the hallowed corridors of this prestigious university.
