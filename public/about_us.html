~   For Malaysians, For Explorers of Malaysia   ~

The Malaysian Students’ Association of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (MSACUHK) was founded in August 2019 by a group of like-minded Malaysian students that felt an association was required to provide a home away from home for Malaysians living in different hostels and residences at CUHK.

Serving as an exploration and learning bridge between the Hong Kong society and the members of its association, MSACUHK is committed to enrich university life for the CUHK Malaysians by organising numerous meaningful events. MSACUHK aims to not only introduce Malaysia culture to the community here but to also foster a sense of unity amongst the Malaysians. MSACUHK also serves as a point of contact between outside parties and Malaysian students in CUHK, which is imperative in the flourishing of students through the joining of external events. With a strong leadership, MSACUHK is poised to grow and bring positive impacts to its members, to the CUHK community and beyond.
