Back to CU!

23 Dec 2020

In lieu of the many members planning their return to Hong Kong at the end of December 2020, we organised an information session to assist and aid our members in making their preparations smoother for the restriction-filled journey back.

Speakers were invited to share their experience on quarantine life after reaching Hong Kong as well as the current on-campus life. Important quarantine measures and requirements were being highlighted to the attendees. Questions and concerns raised by participants before and during the event were also addressed in this session.

The information session was regarded as a highly detailed and informative event by participants. Lastly, we wish to all those coming back, have a safe flight and see you soon!
