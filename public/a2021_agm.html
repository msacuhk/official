MSACUHK 2020/21 Annual General Meeting

25 Sep 2021

The 2nd Annual General Meeting of MSACUHK successfully ended on 25 September 2021. This marked the end of the tenure of the 2nd cabinet and the beginning of the tenure of the 3rd cabinet.

The President of the 2nd Cabinet, Lee Zhi Xuen had presented the Outgoing Committee’s Report and the Treasurer of the 2nd Cabinet, Tan Li Huey had presented the Financial Report during the meeting. The Team Leader of Let’s Rojak Together Video Project, Go Yee Mien had made an introduction of this long-term project to the attendees. The 3rd cabinet passed the majority vote and was duly elected.
