LIFE 1001: Survival After Graduation

7 Jun 2020

This alumni sharing webinar was designed with the objective of shedding some light on the lives of our CUHK Malaysian alumni post-graduation and the different pathways chosen.

Our guest speakers included alumni who started working in Hong Kong and alumnus who pursued postgraduate education in Singapore after graduation. The speakers shared their experiences in chosing their chosen pathways, realitic job expectations, along with some tips on planning your career pathways, how to secure your jobs or postgraduate offer.

Feedback from participants concluded that this initiative was a overall insightful and useful sharing session.
