IN CUHK

The Malaysian Students’ Association of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (MSACUHK) was founded in August 2019 by a group of like-minded Malaysian students that felt an association was required to provide a home away from home for Malaysians living in different hostels and residences at CUHK.
