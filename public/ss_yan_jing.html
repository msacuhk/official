To be honest, I never thought that I would go to university in Hong Kong, not to mention CUHK. However, the first day I stepped into the campus, I knew that I'm in love with this place and the people here. CUHK is really a beautiful campus, with a very diverse culture and very excellent and supportive learning environment. Being a student here for 4 years, I received so much help and support from the professors for my career development, leadership development from college activities and co-curricular activities, and a whole-person development from CUHK's curriculum structure that allowed me to learn something more than just my major studies, preparing me well to enter the society. Despite everything that has happened in these few years, I do not regret my choice of coming to CUHK. Now, changing my identity from CUHK student to CUHK staff, I look forward to how this place can help me to grow and develop professionally!
