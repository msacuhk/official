Malaysian Orientation Day 2019/20

7 Sep 2019

The Malaysian Orientation Day is designed to help Malaysian freshmen get familiarized with the environment at CUHK and aid in their transition to university life.

Through playing various ice-breaking games, we managed to improve bonding between freshmen. A tight-knitted relationship between the freshmen cohort will ensure a staunch and strong communal support within the Malaysian community for whenever they encountered any difficulties.

Besides, we aimed to establish a close relationship between Malaysian seniors and freshmen by including a senior sharing session at the end of the event. Through this segment, freshmen could seek advice from seniors for when they have any doubts during their studies at CUHK.
