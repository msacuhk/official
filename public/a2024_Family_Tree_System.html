Family Tree System

13 Jul 2024

Are you a Malaysian freshie and joining the CUHK family this autumn? Well we know that moving into university can be tough so we have just the system to make that transition easier… (drumroll)…MSACUHK’s Family Tree System🥳!

This system pairs freshies with senior Malaysian students at CUHK to help them learn the ropes around our bustling campus. From academic advice on courses🥱 to which is the best canteen😋, our “parent” students are here to help you ask any questions you might have about uni-life. (P.S you can try asking them to belanja you meals)

The programme also helps connect the our CUHK Malaysian🇲🇾 kampung together, allowing you to get to know your fellow CUHK Malaysians. (You might meet your “Grandparents” if you are lucky😝)
