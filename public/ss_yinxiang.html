I came to Hong Kong to study at CUHK due to the generous scholarship provided. I am grateful for having such a great chance to study at this renowned university. Furthermore, studying at IFAA of CUHK, a course under the business faculty equips me with many business knowledge. This prepares me better for my future career because it helps me realize that actuarial is not only about figures and mathematics. Besides, the living environment in the university, which is about hostel life, gives me a safe place to study in Hong Kong. Moreover, the campus is neither too small nor too big. You can walk the whole campus on your foot, and at the same time, you can find somewhere that has fewer people. Not to mention that the campus is green as well. Despite not having a full four years' experience in CUHK due to the pandemic, the 2+ years of my staying at the university will probably be one of the most unforgettable experiences in my life.
