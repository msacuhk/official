Merdeka Gathering

31 Aug 2020

The Merdeka Gathering was held on the 31st of August 2020, which is the National Day of Malaysia. The purpose of the event was to gather CUHK Malaysian students to celebrate this day together in a leisure manner as we expressed our patrotism and love for our country while being miles away from home.

This year, it was held at the Bagus Bagus Kopitiam, which is one of the famous Malaysian cuisine restaurants in Hong Kong. While having the delicious and reminiscent Malaysian dishes, attendees were free to catch up and relax.
