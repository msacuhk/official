Kia Ora from New Zealand! Going on exchange is one of the most memorable experience in my college life. You can literally play and enjoy every day without worrying about your GPA (but at least pass the courses lah!)

What I love about New Zealand:

Natural beauty: You must visit New Zealand if you’re a nature lover. The snow-capped mountains in Queenstown; the starry starry night in Lake Tekapo; the thundering waterfalls at Milford Sound… more and more are waiting to be explored.

People and lifestyle: Kiwis (people) are friendly and chill. I did enjoy some little moments there: having a scone and hot chocolate early in the morning; visiting farmer’s market for some fresh local food in weekends; exploring New Zealand through bus tours, etc.

Adventure activities: I would say skydiving is a must-try in South Island. The view was absolutely breathtaking – stunning mountain and lake views beneath as I was jumping out of the plane! There is nothing like feeling some fresh air or to be amazed by the beauty of Mother Nature.
