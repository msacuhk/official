Diversity and Inclusion Virtual Fairs

6 & 7 Mar 2021

Supported by CUHK Diversity and Inclusion Office and initiated by Myles Ng, 10 non-local student associations in CUHK had collaborated to present the Diversity and Inclusion Virtual Fairs!

MSACUHK had participated in all four sessions and organised a “Malay for Dummies” session to teach our participants some basic Malay phrases. The weekend was spent with lots of fun and excitement! We were glad to have this opportunity to engage with our event participants from diverse backgrounds.
