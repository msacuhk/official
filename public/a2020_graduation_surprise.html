Senior Graduation Surprise

28 Jul 2020

A 3-minutes short video was curated and shared to the graduating class of 2020 as a token of appreciation and also a small graduation surprise from MSACUHK.

Most of the members have participated in the making of this video alongside the committee members to convey warm messages to the graduating seniors. Pictures of members holding up signs of their well wishes were collected from all across Malaysia as well as Hong Kong, and complied with other relevant materials to create the memorable montage.
