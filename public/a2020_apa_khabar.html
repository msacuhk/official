Apa Khabar?

8 Nov 2020

“Apa Khabar?” literally means “How are you?” in English. This virtual gathering was designed to provide a platform for members to catch up with each other after the mid-term examination period, and to foster a healthy rapport as well as better understanding among the Malaysian community.

Three breakout rooms were created in Zoom’s meeting room to facilitate different activities, namely the “Among Us Room”, “Karaoke Room”, and “Interactive Game Room”. Attendees were given free rein to either stay in the main room to chit chat with others or move freely between each breakout room to partake in different games. This made the event more interactive simulating the ambiance of a physical event.
